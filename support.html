Common Questions

How does bird identification work?

Take a photo or import one from your photo library. The app will identify the bird species for you. You can review and confirm the result before saving it to your collection.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your photos or records to any server.

How do I delete my account?

Birder Diary does not store any data on external servers. Deleting the app from your device removes all your data completely.
