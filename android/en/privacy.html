Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Birder Diary ("we", "our", or "the app") is a bird-watching diary app for Android. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

a) Photos

When you import photos, the app reads image data and EXIF metadata (date, time, GPS coordinates) from your device's photo library. Photos are saved to the app's private local storage for displaying your birding records. We do not upload your original photos to any server.

b) Bird Identification

To identify bird species, the app sends a compressed version of your photo to third-party AI identification services. These images are used only for identifying birds or other species, not for advertising, marketing, or user profiling.

c) Map and Place Data

The app reads GPS coordinates embedded in photo EXIF metadata, or uses a location you manually select, to display birding footprints on a map and convert coordinates into place names. The Android version uses AMap Open Platform map/search SDKs for map display and reverse geocoding. We do not actively access or upload your device's real-time location.

d) User Profile

Your username and avatar are stored locally on your device. They are not transmitted to any server.

2. Data Storage

Your birding records, photo thumbnails, imported photo copies, and profile data are primarily stored locally on your Android device, including app-private storage and the local database. We do not operate a cloud sync service. If you delete the app, local app data is removed by the system.

3. Third-Party Services

  • AMap Open Platform map/search SDKs — used to display birding footprint maps and resolve place names from photo coordinates. AMap SDKs may process device information, network information, and location information. See the AMap Open Platform Privacy Policy.
  • AI identification services — compressed images are used only for species identification. Requests do not include your name, email address, or account information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is only transmitted as described above for core app functionality.

5. Analytics & Tracking

Birder Diary for Android does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or user tracking tools.

6. Children's Privacy

Birder Diary is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Since your primary data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete local data by uninstalling the app. For questions about third-party SDK processing, you can review the relevant privacy policy or contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at feedback@birderdiary.com.
