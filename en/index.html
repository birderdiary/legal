Birder Diary

Snap, identify, and collect birds

Birder Diary is a lightweight and elegant birding journal that turns every encounter with a bird into a page in your own nature notebook.

Photo Identification

Take a photo or import one from your library. Birder Diary identifies the species and gives you its common name and scientific name. Confirm the result and save it to your personal collection with one tap.

Bird Collection

Every sighting is organized by date, like flipping through a nature journal. Over time, you'll build a bird collection that's uniquely yours.

Sighting Map

View your birding adventures on an interactive map. Every location is marked with the birds you spotted there. Relive each trip and discover the nature around you.

Privacy First

All your data stays on your device. Your photos and records are never uploaded to any server.

Whether you're a seasoned birder or just getting curious about the birds around you, Birder Diary is your companion for capturing nature.
